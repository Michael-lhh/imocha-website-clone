iMocha X Workday Recruiting

AI Skills and Agents for Workday HCM Customers

Workday-ready, scalable AI skill agents designed to operate seamlessly across large, global Workday HCM environments—enabling a unified skills language in Workday Skills Cloud and keeping Worker Profiles continuously updated with validated, current skills signals.

See How it Works

The Invisible Layer Behind Skills-Based Transformation

Skills Cloud​

Through bi-directional integration with Workday, iMocha continuously reads skill names and identifiers, enriches them with expanded taxonomy and newly discovered skills, and updates Workday with refined mappings. This ensures skills data remains aligned, current, and enterprise-ready—enabling organizations to rely on a single, continuously improving skills foundation for smarter talent decisions.

Worker Profile​

By inferring skills from resumes, certifications, learning, work, performance signals—and validating them through assessments and AI interviews—iMocha enriches each employee’s skill profile with trusted, evidence-based insights. These validated skills are written back to Workday with proficiency levels and source metadata, ensuring Worker Profile remains current, reliable, and actionable for talent decisions across the organization.

Career Hub​

Thanks to its seamless integration with Workday Career Hub, iMocha enables employees and managers to make career and development decisions using accurate, current skills insights.

Workday Talent Marketplace

iMocha extends Workday Talent Marketplace by bringing reliable skills insights into opportunity matching. With skills context flowing seamlessly into the marketplace, employees are connected to roles, projects, and growth opportunities that align with their capabilities—helping organizations unlock internal mobility and reduce barriers to career progression.

What This Delivers

iMocha works as a complementary intelligence layer that enhances Workday’s talent ecosystem with continuously enriched, high-quality skills data.

Actionable Skills Validation

AI-driven skills inference from resumes, certifications, learning, performance, and real work signals—validated through assessments to keep employee profiles accurate, current, and role-aligned.

The Invisible Layer Behind Workday

A quiet, always-on skills layer that works behind Workday HCM, continuously updating skills data without disrupting employees, managers, or HR workflows.

Skills-Driven Talent Decisions at Scale

Consistent, validated skills data powering internal mobility, career paths, succession planning, and learning recommendations across large, global Workday deployments.
