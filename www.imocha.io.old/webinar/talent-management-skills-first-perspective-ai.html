Innovating Talent Management: A Skills-First Perspective to Workforce Development in the Age of AI

3:30 PM UK Time (8 PM IST) | April 24, 2024

European enterprises are confronting challenges brought on by AI-driven skill shortages, which affect their growth and competitive stance.

This is why, iMocha is inviting industry experts for a dialogue on these issues and the potential of adopting a Skills-first methodology to mitigate them across Europe.

Join us now for a compelling conversation that will cover:

  • The latest insights into Europe's talent landscape and AI's role in evolving job roles.
  • Exploration of a 'Skills-first' approach and its advantages.
  • The importance of Skills Architecture and Skills Mapping for crafting a workforce prepared for the future.
  • The crucial role of Skills Intelligence platforms in executing a 'Skills-first' strategy, and more.
