FAQs for Upskilling and Reskilling

What is Upskilling?

Upskilling is strengthening current skills to stay competitive in evolving roles. For example: A software developer learning cloud computing to build scalable, future-ready applications.

What is Reskilling?

Reskilling is equipping employees with new skills to shift into different roles. For example: A React developer trained in Angular to contribute across diverse front-end frameworks.

Why is upskilling and reskilling critical in today’s workplace?

As job roles evolve rapidly, organizations must continuously align employee capabilities with business needs. Upskilling and reskilling reduce hiring costs, boost retention, and ensure workforce agility.

How does iMocha personalize learning for different employees?

iMocha uses real-time skills data and AI to benchmark current capabilities, identify gaps, and recommend learning paths tailored to each individual’s role, potential, and career goals.

Can iMocha integrate with our existing LMS or LXP?

Yes. iMocha integrates seamlessly with leading LMS, LXP, and HR systems to trigger contextual learning experiences within your existing ecosystem.

How can we measure the success of a reskilling program?

With iMocha, you can track skill development progress, readiness scores, engagement levels, and business impact using intuitive dashboards and analytics.
